Behavioural Scientist | Thought Leader | Critical Thinker.

My name is Krishane Patel and I am a leading behavioural scientist, bringing a unique blend of academic and consulting experience into the field of human behaviour. I am interested in why people behave the way they do, why they can often make decisions that goes against their own interest and how to best combat such problems. Through my work, I delve into the psychological mechanisms that underpin human decision-making, striving to uncover effective strategies for addressing behavioral challenges.

My ultimate goal is to empower individuals and organizations with the knowledge and tools they need to navigate the complexities of human behavior and achieve their goals. To this aim, I have published my blog website which documents useful information for individuals and organisations to understand human behaviour.

Professional Journey

Research Fellow at University of Warwick, leading and managing clinical trial to address workplace mental health disorders.

Consulted on product/service design for a behavioural science startup.
Joined the Financial Conduct Authority as Seniour Associate & Behavioural Insights Lab Manager in the Behavioural Insights Policy Lab.
