About Me

I am a behavioural scientist studying various aspects of human judgment and decision-making processes with respect to behaviour change. My research primarily focuses on the intersection between decision-making and behaviour change. In understanding decision-making processes, we can understand and apply these insights to engineer real long-lasting change. Some of the questions my research is trying to answer:

  • How can we optimally diagnose and understand the causal mechanisms surrounding an issue?
  • How can we optimise people's decisions day-to-day to maximise long term well-being?
  • Do computational methods have anything to offer in behaviour change?
