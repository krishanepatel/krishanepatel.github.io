Understand behaviour

Don't guess what your customers want.
Know them for who they are.

People do not always behave rationally as you may expect them to. For example, why should a patient fail to turn up to their doctors appointment? Why do some consumers spend a large proportion of their income on payday? Understanding people as human means recognising that our decisions are fallible. People's decisions are not always optimal, at times they are influenced by psychological factors such as cognitive biases and heuristics. Targeting these decision processes helps to improve decisions and change behaviour. Krishane's robust practices and research experience can help to deliver effective and strategic change to achieve your business goals.
